# Data Model: Buyer Intelligence Roadmap

## Decision Profile

- Free-text brief
- Must-have, nice-to-have, and deal-breaker text
- Buyer-entered cost scenario

## Market Context Summary

- Area, record count, earliest/latest observation date
- Reported price range and price-per-square-foot range when enough reported values exist

## Saved Research

- Existing query, snapshot, result identifiers
- Derived added, removed, and unchanged identifiers against current results

## Local Feedback

- Result identity, bounded feedback category, timestamp
